Cecil's Bot Mod

Release date: 2021-08-26

This is a mod for classic The Second Encounter that adds Cooperative and Deathmatch player bots. Designed with other mod integration in mind.

You can already try integrating it into your own mod if you're familiar with Serious Sam SDK or try out an Early Access build.
