My Art

Welcome to the gallery of my art

The main purpose of this page is to act as an official archive of all artworks I've made in my life because Twitter's media tab or even Tumblr's archive aren't the most reliable places to store it. Plus this page includes some older art that I never bothered to post anywhere else.

Each piece includes a short description with the context behind the art and links to the respective posts on various platforms, if any.

Click on any image to expand it in full view.

Usage of my art

  • All of my art is protected by copyright law and you are not allowed to use it for anything without my explicit permission.
  • You are allowed to use my art for profile pictures on social media platforms but only if you credit me by either tagging me with my handle on one of the platforms or by leaving a direct link to this site.
  • You are not allowed to repost my art anywhere, even with credit. You may only repost my art by using the "repost" functionality of the respective social media platform, such as retweets on Twitter or reblogs on Tumblr, if I had posted it on any of my accounts.
  • You are not allowed to use any of my work for training any kind of neural network, AI models or other projects of similar nature. This is a violation of the copyright law.
  • Every other use of my art is strictly forbidden.
